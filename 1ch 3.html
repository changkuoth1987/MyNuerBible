Gaat kua̱r ni Dee-bid
1Ti̱ti̱ kɛ gaat Dee-bid tëë da̱pkɛ jɛ mëë tëë kä Ɣɛb-ron. Cu kɛ̱̈ɛ̱̈dɛ a Ɛ̈m-non gat Ä-ɣi-no-am nya wec Yidh-rɛɛl. Kä cua guɔ̱ɔ̱r ɛ Da-ni-yel gat Ä-bi-ga-yil nya Nya-kar-mɛl. 2Kä cua guɔ̱ɔ̱rɛ Ɛ̈b-ca-lom gat Maakaa nya Tɛ̈l-may kuaar Gɛ-cur. Kä cua guɔ̱ɔ̱r ɛ Ä-do-ni-yaa gat Ɣɛ̈-gith. 3Kä cua guɔ̱ɔ̱r ɛ Cɛ-pɛ̈t-yaa gat Ä-bi-tal. Kä cua guɔ̱ɔ̱r ɛ Yith-rɛ-am gat ciekdɛ ni Ɛg-laa. 4Kɛn gaat ti̱ti̱ da̱pkɛ kɛ Dee-bid kä Ɣɛb-ron mëë cɛ ruec thi̱n kɛ run bärɔw wi̱cdɛ päth bäkɛl.
Kä cu Dee-bid ruec kä Jɛ-ru-tha-lɛm kɛ run ti jiɛɛn diɔ̱k wi̱cdɛ diɔ̱k. 5Kä cu ti̱ti̱ a gaat tëë ca da̱p jɛ mëë tëë kä Jɛ-ru-tha-lɛm, cua. Cima kɛnɛ Co-bab kɛ Na-than kɛnɛ Thɔ-lo-mon. Kä cukɛ a kɛn gaat Bɛ̈th-cɛba nya wec A-mi-el.
6Kä cuɛ lɛ tekɛ Yib-ɣar kɛnɛ Ɛli-cama kɛ Ɛ-li-pa-lɛt, 7kɛ No-gaa kɛnɛ Nɛ-pɛg kɛ Ya-pia, 8kɛnɛ Ɛli-cama kɛ Ɛl-yada kɛnɛ Ɛ-li-pa-lɛt. 9Kɛn diaal gaat Dee-bid a /ci gaat luɔɔmnikɛ te thi̱n. Kä cu Ta-mar a nyimandiɛn.
Gaat kua̱r ni Thɔ-lo-mon
10Kä cu gaat Thɔ-lo-mon a Rɛ-ɣɛ̈-bɔm kɛnɛ Ä-bi-yaa kɛ Atha kɛnɛ Yɛ-ɣo-ca-pat, 11kɛ Yo-ram kɛnɛ Ä-ɣɛ̈dh-yaa kɛ Yo-ac, 12kɛ Ä-mä-dhi-ya kɛnɛ Ä-dhar-yaa kɛ Yo-tham, 13kɛ A-ɣadh kɛnɛ Ɣidh-ki-yaa kɛ Ma-na-thɛ, 14kɛ A-mon kɛnɛ Yo-ci-yaa. 15Kä cu gaat Yo-ci-yaa a Yo-ɣa-nan kɛ̱̈ɛ̱̈dɛ kɛnɛ Yɛ-ɣo-ya-kim kɛ Dhid-ki-yaa kɛnɛ Cɛ̈-lum. 16Kä cu gaat Yɛ-ɣo-ya-kim a Yɛ-kɔn-ya kɛnɛ Dhid-ki-yaa.
Gaat kua̱r ni Yɛ-kɔn-ya
17Kä cu ti̱ti̱ a gaat Yɛ-kɔn-ya, mëë ca naŋ ala pɛ̈c ɛ ji̱ Bɛ̈-bi-lɔn Cɛ-ɛ̈l-ti-el, 18kɛ Mal-ki-ram kɛnɛ Pɛ-da-yaa kɛ Cɛn-ɛ̈-dhar kɛnɛ Yɛ-kam-yaa kɛ Ɣo-cama kɛnɛ Nɛ-da-bi-yaa. 19Kä cu gaat Pɛ-da-yaa a Dhɛ-ru-ba-bɛl kɛnɛ Cimi. Kä cu gaat Dhɛ-ru-ba-bɛl a Mɛ-cu-lam kɛnɛ Ɣä-nɛ̈n-yaa. Kä cu nyimandiɛn a Cɛ-lo-mith. 20Kä cuɛ lɛ tekɛ gaat dhieec kɔ̱kiɛn. Kä cukɛ a Ɣä-cu-baa kɛnɛ O-ɣɛl kɛ Bɛ-rɛk-yaa kɛnɛ Ɣä-thad-yaa kɛ Yu-cab-ɣɛ-thɛd.
21Kä cu gaat Ɣä-nɛ̈n-yaa a Pɛ-lɛ̈t-yaa kɛnɛ Yɛ-cɛ̈-yaa, kä cu Yɛ-cɛ̈-yaa a gua̱n Rɛ-pa-yaa min cu la gua̱n Ar-nan gua̱n O-bad-yaa gua̱n Cɛ-kɛ̈n-yaa. 22Kä cu gat Cɛ-kɛ̈n-yaa a Cɛ-mɛ̈-yaa. Kä cu gaat Cɛ-mɛ̈-yaa a. Ɣɛ̈-tuc kɛnɛ Yig-al kɛ Ba-ri-yak kɛnɛ Nɛ-ar-yaa kɛ Ca-pat. 23Kä cu gaat Nɛ-ar-yaa a Ɛl-yo-ɛ-nay kɛnɛ Ɣidh-ki-yaa kɛ Ɛ̈dh-ri-kɔm. 24Kä cu gaat Ɛl-yo-ɛ-nay a Ɣo-dɛ̈w-yaa kɛnɛ Ɛl-ya-cib kɛ Pɛ-la-yaa kɛnɛ Ɛ̈-kub kɛ Yo-ɣa-nan kɛnɛ Dɛ-la-yaa kɛ Ä-nani.
